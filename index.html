A Girl's Journey to Clean Water

Meet Hana, a young girl from Ethiopia. Every day, Hana and her family used to walk for hours to collect water from a distant, unsafe source. The journey was long and tiring, and the water was often dirty.

Thanks to supporters like you, Hana's village now has a clean water well. Now, Hana and her family have safe water right in their community. She can spend more time in school and with her friends, and her family is healthier and happier.

Your donation helps make stories like Hana's possible for families across Ethiopia.
